Beginner-Friendly DIY Wedding Crafts

Simple wedding crafts that anyone can make, even if you're not crafty

You don't need to be a crafting expert to create beautiful wedding decorations. These beginner-friendly projects are simple, require minimal supplies, and produce professional-looking results.

Easy Wedding Crafts for Beginners

1. Ribbon-Wrapped Vases

Simply wrap ribbon around plain vases and secure with hot glue. This instantly transforms basic vases into elegant decor.

  • Time: 5 minutes per vase
  • Skill Level: Beginner

2. Paper Flower Garlands

Use pre-cut paper flowers or templates to create beautiful garlands. String them together with twine or ribbon.

  • Time: 30 minutes per garland
  • Skill Level: Beginner

3. Painted Mason Jars

Spray paint or brush paint mason jars in your wedding colors. Add ribbon, twine, or labels for extra detail.

  • Time: 10 minutes per jar (plus drying time)
  • Skill Level: Beginner

4. Simple Place Cards

Print or hand-write names on cardstock, then fold or cut into elegant shapes. Add a sprig of greenery or ribbon for decoration.

  • Time: 2-3 minutes per card
  • Skill Level: Beginner

Tips for Success

  • Start with simple projects and work your way up
  • Practice on one item before making multiples
  • Don't be afraid to keep it simple â€” less is often more
  • Enlist help from friends and family
  • Give yourself plenty of time before the wedding

Remember, the goal is to create beautiful decorations that reflect your style, not to become a professional crafter. These simple projects will help you achieve that without the stress!
