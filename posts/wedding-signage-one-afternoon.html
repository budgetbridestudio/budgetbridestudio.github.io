Wedding signage is essential for guiding guests and adding personal touches to your celebration. The good news? You can create beautiful, professional-looking signs in just one afternoon â€” no special skills required.

Essential Wedding Signs

1. Welcome Sign

Greet your guests with a personalized welcome sign. Use a large wooden board, mirror, or framed print.

2. Seating Chart

Create an elegant seating chart using a large frame, mirror, or wooden board. List tables and guest names in a clear, readable format.

3. Table Numbers

Simple table numbers can be made with frames, wooden slices, or cardstock. Keep them consistent with your overall design.

4. Directional Signs

Help guests navigate with signs pointing to the ceremony, reception, restrooms, and other important areas.

Quick DIY Methods

  • Stencil Method: Use pre-made stencils and paint for clean, professional lettering
  • Print & Transfer: Print your design and transfer it using transfer paper or chalk
  • Vinyl Decals: Use a Cricut or similar machine to create custom vinyl lettering
  • Hand Lettering: If you have good handwriting, hand-letter your signs for a personal touch

Materials You'll Need

  • Wooden boards, frames, or mirrors
  • Paint (acrylic or chalk paint works best)
  • Brushes or stencils
  • Sandpaper (for distressing, if desired)
  • Clear sealant (to protect outdoor signs)

With these simple techniques, you can create all the signage you need for your wedding in just one afternoon. Start early, work in batches, and don't be afraid to keep it simple!
