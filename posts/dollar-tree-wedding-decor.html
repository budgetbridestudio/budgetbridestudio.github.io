Best Dollar Tree Finds for Weddings

1. Glass Vases and Containers

Dollar Tree's glass vases are perfect for centerpieces. Add some spray paint, ribbon, or flowers, and they'll look completely custom.

2. Candles and Candle Holders

Stock up on pillar candles, tea lights, and votive holders. They're perfect for creating ambiance and work with any wedding style.

3. Ribbon and Fabric

Use Dollar Tree ribbon for chair decorations, table runners, and gift wrapping. The selection changes seasonally, so check back often.

4. Frames and Signage

Simple frames can be painted or decorated to create custom signage. Perfect for welcome signs, seating charts, and table numbers.
